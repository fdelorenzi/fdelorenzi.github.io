About

Engineer · technical leader · founder.

Software engineer and technical leader with over 15 years of experience building and securing products across diverse industries. From hands-on engineering to CTO to founder, always at the intersection of technical leadership, product delivery, and security.

My relationship with software and cybersecurity started in 2005 when I built my first malware removal tool at age 12. It was downloaded over 300,000 times and covered by BBC News, RAIUNO, ANSA, and Panorama. That early experience shaped how I think about building things: solve real problems, ship them, and take security seriously from day one.
