About

Software engineer and technical leader with over 15 years of experience building and securing products across diverse industries. From hands-on engineering to CTO to founder, always at the intersection of technical leadership, product delivery, and security.

My relationship with software and cybersecurity started in 2005 when I built my first malware removal tool at age 12. It was downloaded over 300,000 times and covered by BBC News, RAIUNO, ANSA, and Panorama. That early experience shaped how I think about building things: solve real problems, ship them, and take security seriously from day one.

What I Do Now

Our work spans fractional CTO leadership, dedicated product teams, AI and business automation, custom SaaS development, and vibe-code security audits.
  • I support Vinted as Product Engineer, contributing to high-performance marketplace platform development at scale.
  • I serve as Vice President at PugliaTechs, a nonprofit working to transform Puglia into a global tech hub by connecting startups, investors, institutions, and professionals.
  • I work as a computer forensics consultant for law firms and law enforcement in criminal and civil proceedings, and contribute open-source tools for forensic evidence acquisition and analysis.
